# Interactive Console

We build a tiny console application with JLine3open in new window every release. You may download the example-all.jar from our Release pageopen in new window. Or you can get the lastest snapshot from our workflow artifactsopen in new window: choose the lastest build and find the example artifact.

The jar bundles desktop natives with it, so you should be about to play around with it on your own computer.

Lua Version can be any of 5.1, 5.2, 5.3, 5.4 or jit.
